## MVP Definition

The MVP is **discovery-only**:

- AI-powered matching of users to curated wellness experiences
- Editorial-quality listings with reviews and recommendations
- Waitlist and early-access onboarding flow

**Not in MVP:**
- Booking engine / payments
- Wearable integrations
- User accounts / profiles (beyond waitlist)

## Phase 1 Goals

1. Validate demand — collect 500+ waitlist signups
2. Curate initial supply — onboard 50 premium wellness venues/practitioners
3. Build core matching engine — basic preference-to-experience matching
4. Launch landing page and begin organic + paid acquisition
5. Secure early conversations with 3–5 marquee wellness brands
